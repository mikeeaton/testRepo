How Zintro Works

Submit Inquiry

Client provides a brief description of a project or inquiry and Zintro matches it anonymously with relevant Experts.

Receive Responses

Within a day or two typically 6-12 opted-in Experts reply with a brief explanation of their qualifications and how they can help.

Qualify & Connect

Client and Experts communicate through Zintro to qualify each other, optionally reaching an agreement and connecting.
